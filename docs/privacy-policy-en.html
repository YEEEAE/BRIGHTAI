Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

TL;DR 👀

Look, we get it – privacy policies are usually boring af. So here's the quick version: we respect your data, we don't sell it, and we keep it secure on servers inside Saudi Arabia. We only collect what we need to serve you better. If you want the full legal breakdown, keep scrolling!

What We Collect

We may collect:

  • Contact Info: Name, email, phone when you reach out
  • Business Info: Company name, industry, project requirements
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our site (analytics)
  • Project Data: Whatever you share during our collaboration

How We Use It

  • To deliver the services you signed up for
  • To respond to your questions and support requests
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send important updates (no spam, we promise)

How We Protect It 🔐

Security is literally our thing. Here's how we keep your data safe:

Local Hosting

All data stays in Saudi Arabia. No offshore servers.

AES-256 Encryption

Military-grade encryption for data at rest and in transit.

Access Control

Only authorized team members can access your data.

24/7 Monitoring

Round-the-clock security monitoring and logging.

We Don't Sell Your Data

Let's be crystal clear: we never sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Period. We only share data when:

  • You give us explicit permission
  • It's required by law (we'll fight it if we can)
  • Trusted partners need it to deliver services you requested

Cookies 🍪

Yeah, we use cookies – but only essential ones for site functionality and analytics (Google Analytics). No creepy tracking across the web. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features might not work properly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and view your personal data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Compliance

We comply with Saudi Arabia's National Cybersecurity Authority (NCA) and National Data Management Office (NDMO) standards. Our data practices are designed to meet local regulatory requirements.

Cloud Data & Storage Location

For enterprise and government buyers, we provide a clear data residency model and controlled storage architecture.

  • Primary operational data is hosted in Saudi-based infrastructure whenever required by contract.
  • Backup and disaster recovery locations are contract-defined with explicit customer approval.
  • Encryption in transit and at rest is applied with access auditing controls.
